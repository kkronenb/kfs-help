Cash transactions with no associated checks

Cash receipts with no associated checks (that is, CR documents that record the check amount with the 'TOTAL ONLY' option in the Cash Reconciliation tab) are automatically deposited in the final deposit. For more information about the 'TOTAL ONLY' option, see Cash Reconciliation Tab.
