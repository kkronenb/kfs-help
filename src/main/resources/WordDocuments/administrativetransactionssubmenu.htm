Administrative Transactions Submenu

 Table 184  Administrative Transactions submenu groups

Menu Group

Description

Capital Asset Builder

Allows the compilation of data to build a capital asset from data derived from Accounts Payable and General Ledger processes.

Capital Asset Management

Allows lookup and maintenance of valid values for the attributes needed by the Capital Asset Management module, which tracks information using a series of documents for capital movable/non-movable items and non-capital items. Administrative documents include Asset Manual Payment and Barcode Inventory Process.

Effort Certification

Allows certification, through the recreate document, of the percentage of effort an employee has worked on a particular project represented by a KFS Contracts and Grants account.

Financial Processing

Allows lookup and maintenance of administrative documents that support financial operations, including Cash Management, General Ledger Correction Process, Journal Voucher, Non-Check Disbursements, and Service Billing.

Labor Distribution

 

Allows maintenance of administrative documents that support the Labor Distribution module, including the Labor Journal Voucher and Labor Ledger Correction Process.

System

Allows for the administration of the Electronic Fund Transfer function used for processing receipts and payments.
