Corpus Adjustment

 >  >  >  >

     This document serves as an error correction e-doc for corpus value. Whenever possible, however, it is preferable to reverse and re-enter a transaction that incorrectly indicated the corpus value of an endowment.

The Corpus Adjustment e-doc allows you to record a change in the corpus value of a true endowment that is deemed necessary due to receipt of additional information after the corpus value was recorded in the system. This transaction can only affect the principal for a KEMID.
