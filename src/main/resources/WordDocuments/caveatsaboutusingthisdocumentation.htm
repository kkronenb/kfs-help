Caveats about Using this Documentation

This documentation is specific to the 'out-of-the box' version of KFS 5.0.2, and this out-of-the-box version and your institution's version of KFS may differ in important ways. Consequently, this documentation will not necessarily accurately reflect what you see on your screens, the way your institution uses KFS, or your institution's unique configuration of it.
