Letter of Credit Fund Group

 >  >  >  >

The Letter of Credit (LOC) Fund Group document maintains information pertaining to the various Federal line of credit fund groups available to be linked to an award. A previously non-existing LOC fund group can be added to the table or information for an existing one can be updated. The LOC fund group is primarily used with external sponsoring agencies for Contracts and Grants activities.
