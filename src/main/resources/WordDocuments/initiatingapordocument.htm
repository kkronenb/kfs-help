Initiating a POR Document

 

1   Search for the PO that you want to reopen from the PO search and open the PO.

2   Click open order to reopen the PO and re-encumber any lines that were disencumbered when the PO was originally closed.

 

3        Enter the reason for reopening the PO.

Figure 479 - Enter a reason for reopening the PO

4   Click yes.

The system displays this window.

Figure 480 - Confirmation window

5   Click ok.

The system displays the KFS main menu.
