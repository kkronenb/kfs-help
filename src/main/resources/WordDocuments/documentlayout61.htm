Figure 77 - Asset Payments document

The Asset Payments document includes the Document Overview tab and the Assets tab, which includes sub-tabs of Asset Information, Processed Payments, and Payments Lookup. The document also includes an Accounting Lines tab as well as the standard Notes and Attachments, Ad Hoc Recipients, and Route Log tabs.

For information about the standard tabs, see Standard Tabs in the Overview of the Kuali Financial System.
