Selecting a Vendor's Quote

 

1    After bids have been received, access the PO and appropriately change the quote status for each vendor.

 

Figure 436 – Change each vendor's quote status.

 

 

2    After making your selections, click the complete quote button.

The system displays a confirmation screen.

Figure 437 – Confirmation screen

3   To award the purchase order to the vendor who submitted the successful bid, click yes.

 

After the PO has been awarded to a vendor, the Quote tab becomes display-only and the status of the PO becomes 'In Process'.
