Trial Balance

 > >>

   For information about the standard features of KFS reports, see Standard Reports.

The Trial Balance report combines beginning fund/net equity balances with additions (revenues and other additions), deductions (expenses and other deductions) and ending balance for each account.

Columns for assets and liabilities and cash ownership to complete the accounting equation are also available.
