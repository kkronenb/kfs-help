Deleting Incumbent Funding

To delete incumbent funding:

Click delete for the funding line that you want to mark as delete.

 

Figure 171 – Location of delete button

 

The delete checkbox becomes selected, and the revert and undelete buttons are displayed.

 

Figure 172 – Delete box checked

 

Figure 173 – undelete button displayed

To cancel the operation, click undelete or revert.

 

  revert undeletes and replaces the original values the salary line contained before the delete (available until persisted/saved). undelete simply changes the status of the line to undelete.
