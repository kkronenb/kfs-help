Organization Report Menu

The BC module's organization reports are analysis tools for reconciling and finalizing your base budgets for the next fiscal year. They are highly formatted reports that operate on a set of accounts, positions or incumbents associated with an organization sub-tree. Since organization reports cover larger number of accounts, some reports offer consolidation options (to consolidate sub-accounts into accounts) and a selection list to narrow down the selection of accounts.
