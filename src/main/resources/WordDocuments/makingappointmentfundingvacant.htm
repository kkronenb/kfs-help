Making Appointment Funding Vacant

The vacate button is available when the line is editable, assigned to an employee, not marked delete, and not already vacant. To make appointment funding vacant, follow these steps:

1   Click vacate.

 

Figure 153 – Location of vacate button

 

The Del flag is turned on and the button toggles to revert and undelete button appears.

 

Figure 154 – Results of clicking vacate

2   Click revert or undelete to cancel the operation.

  revert undeletes and replaces the original values the salary line contained before the delete (available until persisted/saved). undelete simply changes the status of the line to undelete.
