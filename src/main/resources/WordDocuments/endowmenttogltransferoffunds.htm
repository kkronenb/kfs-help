Endowment To GL Transfer Of Funds

 >  >  >  >

The Endowment To GL Transfer Of Funds e-doc allows you to move cash balances from one or more KEMID balances to one or more General Ledger accounts. This transaction generates the appropriate General Ledger accounting lines as a part of the KEM-to-General Ledger interface.
