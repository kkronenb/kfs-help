Calculated Salary Foundation Tracker Override

 >>>>

The Calculated Salary Foundation (CSF) Tracker Override table contains data that supersedes data contained in the Labor Distribution Calculated Salary Foundation Tracker table. Overrides are applied from CSF Tracker Override to CSF Tracker as the last step in the process that updates CSF Tracker.

After you select Calculated Salary Foundation Tracker Override from the Maintenance Menu tab, you can look up, create, and modify existing overrides.
