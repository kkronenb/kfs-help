Labor Ledger Pending Entries

Pending entries are collected for labor transactions generated by Labor Ledger e-docs such as Benefit Expense Transfer (BT), Salary Transfer (ST), and Labor Journal Voucher (LLJV). These entries are then passed through the labor scrubber process by the laborNightlyOutJob.

The laborNightlyOutJob generates a summary report of all the transactions collected from the pending table.
