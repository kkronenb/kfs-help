Finalize Deposits Button

     In normal circumstances, the add final deposit button is used to make your final deposits to close out your CMD document. Use the finalize deposits button only if you accidentally deposit all of your checks in the interim deposits screen.

If you accidentally deposit all of your checks in the interim deposits screen, the system displays the finalize deposits button in the bottom of the Deposits tab to allow you to finalize your last interim deposit. The remaining currencies and coins are also finalized.

Click  to finalize the last interim deposit.

When the deposits are finalized, the Cash Drawer Status becomes 'Locked' and you may submit the CMD document.
