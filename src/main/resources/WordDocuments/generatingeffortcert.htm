Generating Effort Certification Reports

Unlike most KFS documents, effort certification reports are not created manually by a KFS user who initiates them one at a time. Effort reports are created via KFS batch processes.

There are two main steps to the process, each associated with a KFS batch job:

1.     Extracting data from the Labor Ledger (the EffortCertificationExtractJob).

2.     Creating effort reports from this extracted data (the EffortCertificationCreateJob).
