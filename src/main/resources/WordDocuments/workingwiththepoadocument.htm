Working with the POA Document

After you create a POA document, you may add, change, or deactivate the existing lines on the original PO.

To add or change the items or accounting lines on the PO, simply add or modify them as you would on the PO document.

To stop further payments on a line item, click the inactivate button for this line item in the Actions column.

Figure 455 - The inactivate button is located in the row for the line item.

After you click the inactivate button, Accounts Payable can no longer make payments on the item. The item line and the accounting line becomes display-only.
