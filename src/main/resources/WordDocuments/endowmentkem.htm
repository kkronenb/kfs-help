Endowment (KEM)

The KFS Endowment module provides a means of accounting for and managing your institution's endowment funds (and similarly invested long-term funds) and their corresponding investments, transactions, obligations, and the underlying funds to which those assets and liabilities belong. It allows you to manage, track and report on investments and investment participation (endowment and non-endowment) and to record and view donor instructions and other fund-related information.

   For detailed information about this module and instructions on working with Endowment functions, see Endowment.
