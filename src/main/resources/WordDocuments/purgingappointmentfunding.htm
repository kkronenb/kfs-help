Purging Appointment Funding

The purge button is available for the position when the salary is editable and contains no CSF values. To purge a position's appointment funding, follow these steps:

Click the purge button.

Figure 155 – Location of purge button

 

The system moves the position to the Purge Appointment Funding tab.

Figure 156 – Position displayed in Purge Appointment Funding tab

Click restore to cancel the operation.
