Budget Construction Selection

>>>>

The Budget Construction Selection option on the Main Menu tab provides a central point of control in the budget construction process. After you choose this option, you may open an existing Budget Construction document or create a new one. Depending on your role and the associated organizational hierarchy, the number of operations available to you via buttons on the screen varies.

     You may use this screen only if Budget Construction is active for one and only one fiscal year.
