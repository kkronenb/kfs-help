Payment Bank History

 >  >  >

The Payment Bank History lookup allows you search for payments by original bank code or current bank code. This allows you to identify payments that may have had their banking information modified. Normally this would only happen if a payment had been cancelled and re-issued and some change had taken place to modify the banking information since the payment was originally disbursed.
