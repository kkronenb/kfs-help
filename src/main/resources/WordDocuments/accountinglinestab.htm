Accounting Lines Tab

The From section of the Accounting Lines tab is populated by selecting Labor Ledger records using the Ledger Balance Importing tab. Amounts in the From section may be edited as necessary as long as the new amount does not exceed the original amount imported from the Ledger Balance Lookup.
