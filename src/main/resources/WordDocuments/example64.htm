Example

A donor establishes an endowment and instructs that the funds generated from it are not to be used until the market value reaches a specific level. The institution sets up an annual tickler to monitor the market value of the KEMID. Additionally, all income paid must be reinvested in principal until the endowment reaches a certain market value, so another annual tickler is set up to monitor the market value for this purpose.
