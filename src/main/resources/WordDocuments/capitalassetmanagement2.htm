Capital Asset Management

 >  >

The KFS Capital Asset Management (CAM) module is used to track assets purchased through the institutional financial system and assets received as gifts, transferred, or even found.

Asset records may be initiated in two ways: through data collected on specific financial transaction documents and through entry on Capital Asset Management documents. Capital Asset Management documents not only allow you to create records; they also allow you to maintain and retire asset records.

Capital Asset Management houses several types of assets within the database. The system may be used to create and maintain assets for art and museum objects, buildings, bonds, infrastructure, land, land improvements, leasehold improvements, library books, and movable equipment. To add non-movable assets to the database, the Add Asset Global and Asset Payment documents are used to create the asset records.

The system also has been designed to allow organizations to create and maintain non-capital asset records. The database records for movable capital assets are created using the Capital Asset Builder.

Capital Asset Management has several documents to assist organizations with the management of assets along with the physical inventory management of assets.

The Asset Edit function allows you to make changes to asset information, including asset detail, asset location, asset organization, land associated with the asset, and any warranty issued for the asset. The document also makes provision for adding new information to asset repair history and asset components.

The Asset Transfer document enables you to transfer ownership of an asset within the institution, while the Loan and Location documents allow you to update the location.

Finally, the Retirement document enables you to record the disposition of an asset. Functionality exists to merge and separate capital assets.

The following table summarizes the CAM lookup and maintenance menu choices.

Capital Asset Management submenu group

Document Type

 

Description

Asset

Presents the Asset Lookup form, which provides access to other functions and documents that are specific to a particular asset: view, edit (CASM), loan/renew/return (ELR), merge, separate, and transfer (AT).

Asset Fabrication (FR)

Used to add records to the asset database for assets constructed by your university or institution.

Asset Global (Add) (AA)

Used to create records in the asset database for assets that are not purchased through the financial system. Also used to create records for non-movable assets that are not processed in the CAB.

Asset Location Global

Used to change location information for multiple assets simultaneously.

Asset Payment (MPAY)

Presents the Payment Lookup from that provides access to other functions and documents that are specific to an particular asset: view, edit, loan, renew, return merge, and transfer. The separate on the payment lookup allows an asset to be separated by a given payment.

Asset Retirement Global (ARG)

Used to change the status of assets and record financial transactions associated with the disposal of capital assets from the university asset database.
