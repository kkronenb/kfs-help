 Lookup and Maintenance Submenu Groups

 Table 5  Lookup and Maintenance submenu

Menu Group

Description

Capital Asset Builder

Allows access to the Pre Asset Tagging document to enable assets to be tagged before other information about the assets has been added to the data base.

Capital Asset Management

Allows users to work in the KFS Capital Asset Management (CAM) module, which is used to track assets purchased through the institutional financial system and assets received as gifts, transferred, or even found. Documents available for general lookup and maintenance include Asset, Asset Fabrication, Asset Global (Add), Asset Location Global, Asset Payment, and Asset Retirement Global.

Chart of Accounts

 

Allows lookup and maintenance of various Chart of Accounts documents that provide key structure to the tracking and storage of financial data through the use of reference tables within the KFS.

Contracts & Grants

Allows creation and lookup of external agency proposals and funding awards necessary in the Contracts & Grants module.

Financial Processing

Allows creation and lookup of travel company names for use on other expense forms through the Voucher Travel Company (DVTC) document.

Vendor

 

Allows for the lookup and creation of a Vendor table to track external businesses or other entities. The Vendor table maintains all information pertinent to each vendor, including tax ID, addresses, contacts and other details required for the procurement process.
