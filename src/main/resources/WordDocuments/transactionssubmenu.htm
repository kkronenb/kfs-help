Transactions Submenu

The Transactions submenu is made up of the following groups of items.

Transactions submenu groups

Menu Group

Description

Accounts Receivable

Enables lookup and maintenance of documents for cash control, customer credit memo, customer invoice, customer invoice write off, customer invoice writeoff (lookup only), and payment application.

Budget Construction

Allows the basic construction of a group of income and expense accounts that create, in combination with other accounts, the financial plan of the organization.

Financial Processing

Allows lookup and maintenance of a variety of general-use documents that support basic financial operations.

Labor Distribution

Allows general maintenance of documents that support the Labor Distribution module, including the benefit and salary expense transfer documents.

Purchasing/Accounts Payable

Allows lookup and maintenance of a variety of general use-documents that support the purchasing and accounts payable processes.

Endowment

Allows lookup and maintenance of endowment transactions for assets, liabilities, cash, security transfers, and transfers to and from the General Ledger.
