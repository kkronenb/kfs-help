Closing a Travel Authorization

To close a Travel Authorization:

1.     Open the Travel Authorization document.

        If the Travel Authorization has been amended, then you would open the Travel Authorization Amendment whose status is Open for Reimbursement.”

2.     Click the button. The system displays the message, “When you close this Travel Authorization, remaining encumbrance will be liquidated”.

3.     To accept the message, click . The system displays the Travel Authorization Close document.

        The Travel Authorization Close document Status is Final, the TAC Status is Closed. The Notes and Attachments tab includes the note, “This Travel Authorization was closed by [your name]” in addition to any other notes from related TA and TAA.

        If Travel Authorization/Travel Authorization Amendment status becomes Retired Version and this warning displays in the upper left corner: “Warning: This is not the current version of this Travel Authorization”. The Notes and Attachments tab includes the note, “This Travel Authorization was closed by [your name]” in addition to any other notes from related TA and TAA.
