Monthly Budget Management

Creating Month Budget

The create month button causes the system to display the Budget Construction Monthly screen for the currently selected accounting line. This screen in edit mode allows you to edit each of the monthly request amounts and optionally to return a new annual amount based on the sum of the monthly amounts.

To access this screen and begin work, follow these steps:

1   Click create month.

The system displays the BC Monthly screen.

Figure 107 – BC Monthly screen

2   Enter the monthly budget or click month spread. 

Clicking the month spread button equally divides the monthly budget into each period. If the amount is not evenly divisible to the whole dollar, the first month receives the remainder.

Figure 108 – Result of clicking the month spread button

3   Click save when you are done.

When you next return to the BC document, notice that the create month button has changed to edit month.

Figure 109 – Display of edit month button

 

4   If you wish to delete the monthly budget:

a,  Click month delete.

b.  Confirm that you wish to delete the record.

Figure 110 – Confirm deletion

Spreading Month Globally

Instead of spreading month budget one at a time, you may spread the monthly budget globally by using the month spread button.

To spread the budget monthly on all budgeted lines, click month spread.

Figure 111 – Click the month spread button.

 

The edit month and view month buttons appear on each line.

Figure 112 – Display of edit month and view month buttons
