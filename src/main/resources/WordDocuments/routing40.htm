Routing

Fiscal Officer for all Credit and Debit accounts must approve the AV document. Additional approvals may be established within the institution's review hierarchy or are designated through Ad Hoc routing. The document status becomes 'FINAL' when the required approvals are obtained and the transaction is posted to the G/L during the next G/L batch process.
