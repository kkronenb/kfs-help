Campus

 >  >  >  >  >

The CampusMaintenanceDocument is used to identify the different fiscal and physical operating entities of an institution for use in the KFS. A campus may be identified as a fiscal entity, a physical entity, or both.

When the user chooses the Campus option, the system displays the Campus Lookup screen. After the user selects a campus or clicks the create new button, the system presents the CampusMaintenanceDocument.
