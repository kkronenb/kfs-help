Blanket Approval

Authority that is given to designated Reviewers|keyword=Reviewer who can approve a document to a chosen route point. A Blanket Approval bypasses approvals that would otherwise be required in the Routing|keyword=Routing. For an authorized Reviewer, the Doc Handler|keyword=Doc Handler typically displays the Blanket Approval button along with the other options. When a Blanket Approval is used, the Reviewers who are skipped are sent Acknowledge requests to notify them that they were bypassed.
