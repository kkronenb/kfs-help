KFS Modules

This section introduces the major software modules that constitute the KFS. These modules include Chart of Accounts (COA), Vendor, Purchasing and Accounts Payable (PURAP), Pre-Disbursement Processor (PDP), Capital Asset Management (CAM), Accounts Receivable (AR), Contracts and Grants (C&G), Effort Certification, and Labor Distribution.
