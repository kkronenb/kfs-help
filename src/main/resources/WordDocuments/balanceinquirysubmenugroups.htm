Balance Inquiry Submenu Groups

Table 127  Balance Inquiries submenu

Menu Group

Description

General Ledger

 

Enables retrieval of budgets, pending entries, actual amounts posted and other transactional data within the General Ledger that are combined into the following documents: Available Balances, Balances by Consolidation, Cash Balances, General Ledger Balance, General Ledger Entry, General Ledger Pending Entry, and Open Encumbrances.

Labor Distribution

 

Allows retrieval of salary and benefit data from various modules for inquiries of account status (base funds), account status (current funds), calculated salary foundation, employee funding, july 1 position funding, labor ledger view, labor ledger pending entry, and position inquiry.
