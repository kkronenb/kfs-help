Document Layout

The document layout of the POA document is identical to that of the original PO, with the addition of a notation regarding status in the Document Header. A notation also displays at the top of the PO document, such as 'Warning: There is a pending action on this Purchase Order.'

   For information about the fields on this document, see Docment Layout under Purchase Order.
